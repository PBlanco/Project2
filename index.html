The graph to the side is a representation of top Billboard Songs in the last 24 years.

The popularity of the song is based off of Lancefer's scoring system where points are awarded for each week the song is on the chart, and by how high it is ranked on the billboard.

Click on a circle to see more information about the specific song and to isolate other songs by the artist. To reset the graph, simply click on the white space in the graph. You may also drag the information box around in case it is covering certain data points.

Drag the slider to change the timeline of songs displayed.

Use the search box below to find specific songs. Note: It is case sensitive.
